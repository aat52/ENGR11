My Future as a Professional Engineer

Becoming a Professional Engineer is to reach the highest level of competence in engineering. To be recognized as one, you must graduate from an ABET-accredited engineering program, pass the Fundamentals of Engineering (FE) exam, and work as an engineer for four years. After this, you must pass the Professional Engineering (PE) exam.Through this hard work, you gain higher chances of employment, respect from peers and employers, and a sense of accomplishment. Professional Engineers are respected by the public because they have proven to excel in the ethical professional practices that engineers must follow.
